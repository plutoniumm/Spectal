Get to Know Us.

Spectal Management is a boutique talent management agency with event management as its parallel vertical. At present, we are managing Hindi rock band 'The Local Train'.

We undertake a spectrum of duties from managing bookings to overseeing artists’ PR and other engagements; strategically helping in augmenting and solidifying artists' career. Spectal has expansively marked its presence by planning and organising some of the most prominent university festivals across India.

Alongside our management vertical, we take immense pride in creating customised promotional campaigns for niche brands with an aim to expand their reach and better their identity in the market.

We are a young organisation that aims to create experiences that transform talent as well as audiences across the globe. We go above and beyond the expectations and deliver our work with utmost diligence and sincerity.
